How to make Nigerian Garri

Description

Garri is a staple food for many Nigerians

It is grinded cassava and is a good source of carbs

Ingredients

  • Garri Powder
  • Sugar
  • Groundnuts
  • Water

Steps

  1. Pour the garri powder into a bowl
  2. Add your Groundnuts and sugar
  3. Add your desired level of water
  4. Drink and enjoy
