How to make a Nigerian Bean Porridge

Description

Nigerian bean porridge is made using a “Honey Beans”

Honey beans can be found in West Africa

Ingredients

  • Honey beans
  • Palm Oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Red onion
  • Bell pepper
  • Ground Shrimp
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Rinse the beans and place in a deep pot
  2. Boil the beans on medium heat until it becomes soft
  3. While the beans is boiling,blend the tomatoes,peppers,and onions
  4. Once the beans are softened from boiling,add the blended tomatoes to it
  5. Allow the beans to cook for additional 10 minutes, then add the crayfish, salt and other seasonings you might prefer
  6. Turn off heat and let cool before serving
