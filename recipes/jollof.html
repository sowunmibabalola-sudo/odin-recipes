How To Make Jollof Rice

Descriprion

Jollof Rice is made using natural and flavourful ingredients

These natural ingredients give this dish a very unique flavor

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Red Bell Peppers
  • Onions
  • Rice
  • Tomato Paste
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Blend together the tomatoes, bell peppers, and onions
  2. Diced some onions and heat up your prefered oil
  3. Let Onions fry for 2 minutes on low heat
  4. Add the blended pepper mix to the fried onions
  5. Throughly wash the rice while the pepper sauce is cooking
  6. Add the washed rice to the pepper mix and cook on low heat for 45 minutes
  7. Add your preferred seasoning
  8. Enjoy when done
